About

ALT+F4 was originally created as a social hub for the guidless, then it hit us: why not form our own guild?

We're new to the Black Desert Online scene but we have veterans of the MMO genre and we're ambitious. Our goals: explore the world, slay some big-ass monsters, become famous crafters, and even claim some territory.
